
Bob is appointed as an invigilator for an examination. In the examination hall there are NN students with roll number 11 to NN and NN benches. The seating arrangement is as follows: first all odd roll number students are seated and then all even roll number students. Bob wants to know the roll number of the student sitting on KthKth bench. Since Bob is weak at maths he needs your help.

Input
The only line of input contains integers NN and KK.

Output
Print the roll number of the student sitting on KthKth bench.

Constraints
1≤K≤N≤10121≤K≤N≤1012

Sample Input
12 5
Sample Output
9
EXPLANATION:
The sequence of roll numbers will be 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 11, 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12. The roll number at fifth place is 9.
