
Chef, top cook of Chefland decided to host a cooking contest to spot the emerging cooks of Chefland.
It is a team contest,each team consists of exactly two cooks. Each team should have a cooking skill equal to KK. Cooking skill of a team is the sum of the cooking skills of its cooks.
There are NN participants for the contest,each with cooking skill AiAi.
Chef has to make the necessary arrangements for the contest , so you have to help him to find out the maximum no. of teams which can be formed.
Input:

First line will contain TT, number of testcases. Then the testcases follow. 
Each testcase contains two line of input, two integers N,KN,K. 
The next line contains NN space separated integers AiAi denoting the cooking skill of ithith participant.  

Output:
For each testcase, output in a single line the maximum no. of teams that can participate in the contest.
Constraints

1≤T≤1001≤T≤100
2≤N≤1052≤N≤105
2≤K≤1092≤K≤109
1≤Ai≤1091≤Ai≤109

Sample Input:
2
4 5
1 3 4 2
3 4
2 1 4  
Sample Output:
2
0  
EXPLANATION:
TestCase1:TestCase1:
Clearly 1st1st & 3rd3rd participant and 2nd2nd & 4th4th can form the team.
So 22 teams are formed.
