
There are NN men and MM women in a theatre club. They need to select a group of exactly KK actors comprising at least 4 men and at least 1 woman to set a play "Hamlet". How many ways of making a choice for a group are there?

Input
The only line of input contains three integers NN, MM and KK.

Output
Print the required number of ways.

Constraints

4≤N≤304≤N≤30
1≤M≤301≤M≤30
5≤K≤N+M5≤K≤N+M
Example Input 1
7 3 6
Example Output 1
168
Example Input 2
4 4 6

Example Output 2
6
